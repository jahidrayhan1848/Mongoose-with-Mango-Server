Child Protection and Safeguarding Policy

Date Adopted: 7th June 2025 | Review Date: June 2026

Policy Lead: Tracie Daly, DSL | Approved by: Mark Aitken

1. Statement of Intent

ElevatEd AP Ltd is committed to safeguarding and promoting the welfare of all children and young people...

2. Guiding Principles

  • Child-Centred Approach: We will always act in the best interests of the child.
  • Prevention: We will take proactive steps to prevent harm to children.
  • Protection: We will respond robustly and effectively to concerns about child abuse or neglect.
  • Partnership: We will work collaboratively with parents/carers, local authorities, and other agencies to safeguard children.
  • Confidentiality: Information will be shared on a "need to know" basis.
  • Transparency: Our procedures will be clear and accessible.

3. Scope and Definitions

Scope: This policy applies to all children and young people attending ElevatEd...

  • Child Protection: Protecting children from maltreatment...
  • Safeguarding: Encompasses child protection and broader welfare...
  • Abuse Categories: Physical, Emotional, Sexual, Neglect
  • Harmful Sexual Behaviour: Differentiating between children & adults
  • Contextual Safeguarding: Extra-familial risks

4. Roles and Responsibilities

Designated Safeguarding Lead (DSL): Tracie Daly (tracie@elevate-ed.co.uk)

  • First point of contact for all safeguarding concerns.
  • Liaises with social care & agencies.
  • Maintains confidential records & advises staff.

All Staff & Volunteers: Familiarise with this policy & KCSIE, report concerns, maintain professional boundaries...
