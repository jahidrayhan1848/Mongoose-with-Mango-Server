Elevate Nutrition Coaching

Transform your relationship with food through personalized strategies, practical tips, and ongoing motivation to help you thrive.

Program Features

Personalized Nutrition Assessment

Evaluation of your current eating habits, dietary preferences, lifestyle, and nutritional needs to tailor recommendations.

Meal Planning

Sharing information about healthy food groups, portion sizes, and dietary guidelines, and recommending healthy recipes that are easy to make and affordable.

Behavior Change Support

Strategies to establish and sustain healthy eating habits, overcome emotional or mindless eating, and create lasting lifestyle changes.

Meal Preparation & Cooking Tips

Practical advice on meal prep, healthy cooking techniques, and incorporating more whole foods into your diet.

Tracking & Accountability

Use of food journals, apps, or online tools to monitor intake, moods, and progress, with regular check-ins to evaluate success and make adjustments.

Support for Specific Goals

Guidance tailored to weight management, managing food sensitivities, improving energy levels, or optimizing gut health.

Addressing Challenges

Identifying barriers to healthy eating, such as stress, emotional triggers, or social situations, and developing strategies to overcome them.

Motivational Coaching

Encouragement, motivation, and accountability to keep you engaged and committed to your health journey.

Monthly Membership Program Includes

Monthly Health Coach Session

Personalized one-on-one coaching sessions to track progress and set new goals.

Nutritious Recipes & Meal Planning

Access to healthy recipes, meal planning resources, and meal prep strategies.

Food & Mood Journals

Track eating habits and emotional health to better understand patterns and triggers.

Engaging Challenges

Participate in fun challenges designed to keep you motivated and on track.

Ongoing Support

Stay connected through secure messaging with your coach between sessions.

Elevate Nutrition Coaching is designed to empower you with the knowledge, tools, and motivation to transform your nutrition habits. For personalized guidance aligned with your unique health needs, always consult a qualified healthcare professional.
