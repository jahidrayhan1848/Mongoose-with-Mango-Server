Supporting Schools and Young People

At ElevatEd, we work hand-in-hand with schools, trusts, and local authorities to provide a tailored, wellbeing-focused alternative provision for young people aged 11–14. Our holistic approach supports academic progress, personal development, and emotional resilience, ensuring every learner has the tools to thrive both in school and beyond.

We offer flexible placements, part-time learning, and bespoke programmes across our five pillars—Wellbeing, Leadership, Sport, Arts, and Achievement—designed to re-engage pupils, improve attendance, and foster confidence.

By partnering closely with schools, we create seamless support pathways, providing regular updates, collaborative planning, and shared strategies that strengthen each child’s journey. Our goal is simple: to transform the educational experience for every young person we work with, enabling them to achieve their potential and flourish in all areas of life.
