How We Work

“Utilising the ElevatEd Assessment System (based on the safeguarding and promoting safeguarding framework) we assess the individual needs of each child and create an action plan including setting realistic and achievable targets which are monitored, reviewed and used to support the young person’s journey with us. Through the assessment process we are also able to create supportive pathways within the community to support the young person’s transition back into the school setting.”
