About Us

How we work?

Welcome to ElevatEd. We provide an alternative provision focused on wellbeing, leadership, sport, arts and achievement. Our programmes are child-centred, delivered in a calm, spacious venue where pupils aged 11–15 can rebuild confidence, re-engage with learning and progress socially and academically. We work closely with families and schools to create tailored plans and use both on-site resources and local community facilities to deliver a broad and meaningful curriculum.

Parents

At ElevatEd, we understand that your child’s education is a partnership. We’re here to support you and your child every step of the way, offering a safe, nurturing environment where young people aged 11–15 can rediscover their confidence and love for learning.

Our safeguarding commitment and tailored support ensures that pupils receive individualised attention. We work closely with families to create consistent approaches across home and provision.
