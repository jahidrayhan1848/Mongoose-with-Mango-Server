What Are Peptides?

Peptides are short chains of amino acids (2-50) that regulate processes like tissue repair, hormone production, and immune response. There are endogenous peptides our bodies naturally produce, and exogenous peptides sourced externally to supplement or enhance these functions.

What is Peptide Therapy?

This treatment involves administering specific peptides to improve or support bodily functions when natural production is insufficient. Peptide therapy can target issues like weight management, muscle growth, anti-aging, hormonal balance, immune support, skin health, sexual vitality, and more.
