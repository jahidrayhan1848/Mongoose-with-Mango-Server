Services for Menopause & Perimenopause Care

Symptom Management

Address hot flashes, night sweats, mood changes, sleep disturbances, and hormonal fluctuations with tailored treatment plans.

Hormone Assessments

Conduct and interpret hormone testing to inform individualized therapy options.

Lifestyle & Nutrition Counseling

Personalized advice on diet, exercise, stress management, and sleep hygiene to promote balance and wellness.

Innovative Therapies

Including hormone replacement therapy (HRT), non-hormonal alternatives, herbal medicine, nutritional supplements, and integrative modalities.

Health Coaching & Education

Empowering you with tools, strategies, and education to enhance self-care during this transition.

Follow-up & Monitoring

Ongoing support, treatment adjustments, and progress evaluation to ensure continued well-being.

Labs

Your initial visit includes baseline tests such as CBC, CMP, and a Thyroid panel. Additional tests depend on your history, type of HRT, and provider’s recommendations. Please note: extra tests beyond the baseline will incur an additional cost.
